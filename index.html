¡Hola and Welcome to Puentes!

Welcome to Puentes! Here we have very effective, informative and simple lessons. Our goal here is to introduce you to basic spanish grammar with concise explanations and tables with an easy to navigate site. We aren’t going to overcomplicate things or go off topic. We are here to help you. Our website contains lessons explaining each tense and how to communicate in each one. We also have an email where you can ask us specific questions about spanish and we will respond within 2-3 days or sooner if possible. Our website is helpful for learners of any age and of any background. The only thing you need to learn is determination and persistence. We strive to make learning spanish fun, yet simple. We take your experience very seriously. If youre more comfortable with spanish, you can change the language in the top right corner. If not, our learning pages are listed under grammar pages.
